About Me

I'm based in Duffy, ACT
I print custom mechanical parts quickly and cheaply.
I can print your model or I can create a model for you.
I can model from a broken part.
For more info on me go to nathan.woodburn.id.au

Services

3D Printing

3D Printers

I use a Flying Bear Ghost 3D printer for ABS and TPU
It prints at a maximum of 150 x 150 x 150 mm
I may be able to print bigger models by splitting them

I use a Phrozen Sonic Mighty 4K for resin prints
It prints at a maximum of 100 x 150 x 150 mm

Materials

I print in ABS plastic for Mechanical parts or TPU plastic for flexible parts
Both ABS and TPU have a high melting point of approx. 220 °C
I print in resin for intricate parts and miniature models
Please contact me if you would like other materials

Software

I design my CAD models using Autodesk Fusion 360.
I use Ultimaker CURA for slicing my FDM 3d prints.
I use CHITUBOX for slicing my resin prints.
I use 3D Print Log to record all my prints and filament usage
