Pricing


3D Printing pricing is based on the end product's weight,
the chosen material and the printing time.

ABS+ $0.15/g + $2/hr
ABS $0.15/g + $3/hr
TPU $0.30/g + $3/hr
Resin $0.60/g + $2/hr + post-processing of $5/print

Minimal order $5 for ABS/PLA
Minimal order $10 for Resin
Additional colours can be ordered in.

Cad
Pricing

CAD (Computer Aided  Design) pricing is based on the time it takes to design and the complexity of the design

Quick and easy designs such as this vacuum nozzle adapter cost approx. $5
Slower and more difficult designs such as this airer connector or gear cost approx. $10
Hard to measure designs such as this head torch mount cost approx. $15

All CAD designs come with .stl files and Fusion 360 Files.
